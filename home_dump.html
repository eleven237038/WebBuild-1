UNDERSTANDING PEPTIDE PURITY & TESTING

Peptide purity is determined through High-Performance Liquid Chromatography (HPLC), the gold standard in analytical chemistry. This method separates compounds based on their molecular properties, allowing precise quantification of the target peptide versus impurities. A purity level of 99% means that out of every 100 molecules, 99 are the intended compound — a standard that exceeds typical industry benchmarks.

Mass spectrometry (MS) complements HPLC by confirming molecular weight and structural integrity. Together, HPLC and MS form a complete analytical framework that ensures researchers receive exactly what they ordered. At InsightWellnessLab, every batch is independently verified and documented before release.

Proper storage conditions — typically lyophilized powder at -20°C — preserve peptide integrity for long-term stability. Always consult the included COA for batch-specific handling recommendations.
